James LaRue Avery

One of the most loved TV Dad

  • 1945 - born in Pughsville, Virginia
  • 1968 - served In Vietnam War
  • 1976 - earned degree in Bachelor of Arts in drama and
    literature from Thurgood Marshall College
  • 1980s - begins career in acting
  • 1990s - gains prominence for his role as
    Uncle Phil in the "Fresh Prince of Bel Air".
    Other notable voice credits of his includes
    Shredder in the original Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles and
    James Rhodes/War Machine in 1990s Iron Man
  • 2007/2012 - was commencement speaker for his alma mater
    UC San Diego's Thurgood Marshall College
  • 1988 - marries Barbara, has no children of his own
  • 2013 - dies from complications from open heart surgery

"Some of my greatest lessons in Acting, Living and being a respectable human being came through James Avery. Every young man needs an Uncle Phil. Rest in Peace"


-- Will Smith
